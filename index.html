Welcome to Mehul Interior

Mehul Interior is a Construction Company that has built a strong reputation for delivering a quality service, on time, and at a competitive price. We pride ourselves on being skilled and experienced in all aspects of Civil, Electrical and Plumbing.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence invariably results in a successfully completed project for both Contractors and clients. We understand and promote the idea of working as a partnership with our clients to ensure their goals are met.

At Mehul Interior we never rest upon our laurels and are passionate and driven to continue the high levels of customer satisfaction we have achieved over the past 2 years.

We undertake a variety of projects for a wide range of clientele – from small private developments to large Developers and Corporate projects. Our unique and flexible project management systems ensure that a positive outcome is achieved regardless of size or nature of the project.

With a short and intimate history, it is no wonder the culture at Mehul Interior is one of continual improvement. With this “can-do” attitude we are excited to confront the challenges and seize the opportunities the future will bring.

From a humble beginning with an Annual turnover of rs. 50 lakhs in Fy 2020-21 to approx Rs 1 Crores in FY 2021-22 to Rs. 7 Crores running projects , we are expanding our business at more than 2 X in terms of Financials.
